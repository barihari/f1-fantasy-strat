## Circuit Analysis

### Track Characteristics
- **Length:** 5.3km, 58 laps
- **Overtake Zones:** Turn 1 braking zone, DRS straights (now X-Mode for all cars), turn 9-10 chicane
- **Key Features:** Elevation changes, mix of high/low speed corners, semi-permanent barriers
- **Historical Avg:** 40-45 overtakes per race

### 2026 Regulation Impact
- Active aero X-Mode on straights eliminates DRS proximity dependency — every car gets low-drag mode
- Energy management critical with 50/50 power split — teams with poor battery deployment will fade in final stint
- Race starts more volatile (no MGU-K first 50m) — expect more first-lap position changes than historical
- Reduced downforce may increase overtaking opportunities, but Melbourne's medium-speed nature limits impact

### Weather Forecast
**Current:** Partly cloudy, 20°C. **Rain probability: 25%.** Forecast stable but Melbourne weather can change quickly. No wet weather preparation recommended.
