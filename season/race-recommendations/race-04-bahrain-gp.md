## Circuit Analysis

### Track Characteristics
- **Length:** 5.412km, 57 laps
- **Overtake Zones:** Turn 1 heavy braking zone, back straight to Turn 4, Turn 11 exit onto main straight
- **Key Features:** Multiple long straights, heavy tire degradation, favors raw power over aero efficiency
- **Historical Avg:** 50+ overtakes per race (high tier)

### 2026 Regulation Impact
- Active aero on every straight will increase overtake opportunities beyond historical 50+ baseline
- Energy management critical on this power-sensitive circuit — teams with superior battery deployment will have hidden pace advantage
- 50/50 power split means electrical deployment strategy more important than pure ICE power
- First lap starts without MGU-K for 50m creates more wheelspin risk in desert heat

**Current:** Clear, 30°C ambient. **Rain probability: 0%.** Desert circuit with zero weather variance — pure performance weekend.
