# F1 Fantasy 2026 Game Rules

## Team Composition
- Pick **5 drivers** and **2 constructors**
- Stay within the **$100M cost cap**
- You can manage up to 3 teams, but only 1 matters for competitive play
- All teams are eligible for Global League prizes (new for 2026)

## 2X Boost
- Assign to **one driver** each race weekend
- Doubles their entire weekend score (Qualifying + Race, or Qualifying + Sprint + Race on Sprint weekends)
- Can be moved to any driver on your team before lineup lock
- After a chip weekend, the boost stays on whoever had it last — remember to move it back

## Transfers
- **2 free transfers** before each race
- **1 unused transfer carries over** to the next race (maximum 3 banked)
- Each additional transfer beyond the free allowance costs **-10 points**
- **New for 2026:** Transfers are calculated based on the **net change** from your previous lineup, not each individual swap. If you swap a driver out and back in, it costs 0 transfers.

## Price Changes
- Driver/constructor prices update after each race based on performance
- Prices can go up or down based on a 3-race rolling PPM (Points Per Million) average
- **Price floor reduced to $3M** (new for 2026, down from $4.5M)
- See `knowledge/pricing-algorithm.md` for the full model

## Chips (Power-Ups)
- 6 chips available, each usable **once per season**
- Only **one chip per race weekend**
- 3 chips available from the start; 3 more unlock after completing Race 1
- Once activated, chips **cannot be cancelled**
- See `knowledge/chip-strategy.md` for full details

## Lineup Lock
- Lineups lock before the first session of the weekend
- On standard weekends: before FP1 (Friday)
- On Sprint weekends: before the Sprint Qualifying session (Friday)
- The Final Fix chip allows one driver swap after the first scoring session

## Leagues
- **Global League** — compete against all players worldwide
- **Mini-Leagues** — public or private leagues with friends
- **Head-to-Head Battlemode** — race-by-race competition against a specific opponent

## Key 2026 Rule Changes Summary
1. Sprint DNF penalty reduced to -10 (was -20)
2. Transfers based on net changes (not individual swaps)
3. Price floor reduced to $3M
4. All teams eligible for Global League prizes
5. New team on grid: Cadillac (11th constructor)
