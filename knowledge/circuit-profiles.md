# F1 Fantasy 2026 Circuit Profiles

Overtake tiers based on 3-year average data. Strategy implications for each circuit.

---

## Round 1: Australian GP — Melbourne (Albert Park)
- **Type:** Semi-permanent street circuit
- **Overtake Tier:** Medium
- **Sprint:** No
- **Weather Risk:** Medium (March autumn, occasional rain)
- **Key Stats:** 40-45 overtakes avg, multiple DRS zones historically, elevation changes
- **Fantasy Strategy:** Balanced picks. Overtakers and qualifiers both viable. Season opener — limited data, go with pre-season testing form.
- **Historical Performers:** Verstappen (3 wins in 4 years), Leclerc (consistent podiums)
- **2026 Intel:** Leclerc fastest in testing. Aston Martin planning early retirements (avoid). Bottas has 5-place grid penalty (overtake upside).

## Round 2: Chinese GP — Shanghai (Sprint)
- **Type:** Permanent
- **Overtake Tier:** High
- **Sprint:** Yes
- **Weather Risk:** Low
- **Key Stats:** Long back straight, 50+ overtakes avg, favors raw pace
- **Fantasy Strategy:** Sprint weekend = more scoring sessions. High overtakes favor aggressive picks. First Sprint of season — 3X Boost too early (not enough data on form).
- **Historical Performers:** Verstappen, Hamilton, Norris
- **Chip Note:** Avoid using 3X here (too early). Save for later Sprints.

## Round 3: Japanese GP — Suzuka
- **Type:** Permanent
- **Overtake Tier:** Medium-High
- **Sprint:** No
- **Weather Risk:** Medium (spring rain possible)
- **Key Stats:** Technical circuit, ~40 overtakes, rewards car balance and driver skill
- **Fantasy Strategy:** Favor drivers from teams with strong aero packages. Constructors with both drivers in Q3 earn big bonuses here.
- **Historical Performers:** Verstappen (dominant), Norris, Piastri

## Round 4: Bahrain GP — Sakhir

- **Risk:** GEOPOLITICAL — may be cancelled/relocated. Do NOT anchor chips here.
- **Historical Performers:** Verstappen, Leclerc, Hamilton

## Round 7: Canadian GP — Montreal (Sprint)

## Round 8: Monaco GP — Monte Carlo
- **Type:** Street circuit
- **Overtake Tier:** Very Low
- **Sprint:** No
- **Weather Risk:** Low-Medium
- **Key Stats:** Fewest overtakes on calendar (<15), qualifying IS the race, crashes common
- **Fantasy Strategy:** **LIMITLESS PRIORITY TARGET #1.** 48.6% of Top 500 used Limitless here in 2025. Pick the 5 best qualifiers + 2 strongest constructors regardless of budget. Avoid overtake-dependent value picks.
- **Historical Performers:** Leclerc (pole specialist), Verstappen, Sainz
- **Chip Note:** Limitless. Overwhelming consensus among top players.
